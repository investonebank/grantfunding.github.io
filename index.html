A free grant is money from the government or a foundation that is given to people who will use the money to improve their lives. Each year billions of dollars are given away, and every citizen is eligible to apply. Imagine getting the money you need to do what you’ve just dreamed of. Bad Credit History? Bankruptcy? You Still Qualify!

Why Would The Government and Foundations Want to Give You Funding?

The federal government every year allocates billions of dollars to various agencies, and it has to be spent. Plus, charities and foundations are legally obligated to give away a portion of their money every year, to keep their tax status. They want and need people (like you!) who will use their money now so that they can get more next year, to give away. If they don’t use it, they lose it, so they’re always looking for qualified people who need money to use for a business, education, real estate, and dozens of other purposes.
